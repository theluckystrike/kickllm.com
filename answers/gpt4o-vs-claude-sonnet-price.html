GPT-4o vs Claude Sonnet Price Comparison

GPT-4o: $2.50/$10.00 per 1M tokens. Claude Sonnet 4: $3.00/$15.00. GPT-4o is cheaper on input, same on output. For most workloads, GPT-4o costs 15-20% less.

Side-by-Side Pricing

Context window128K tokens200K tokens
ProviderOpenAIAnthropic

Cost at Scale

Assuming a typical workload of 1M tokens/day (70% input, 30% output):

  • GPT-4o: (700K × $2.50 + 300K × $10.00) / 1M = $4.75/day = ~$143/month
  • Claude Sonnet 4: (700K × $3.00 + 300K × $15.00) / 1M = $6.60/day = ~$198/month

GPT-4o saves about $55/month (~28%) at this usage level.

When to Choose Each

  • Choose GPT-4o when cost is the primary factor, you need vision capabilities, or your workload is output-heavy
  • Choose Claude Sonnet 4 when you need a larger context window (200K vs 128K), longer-form outputs, or prefer Anthropic's safety approach
  • Choose neither if your task doesn't need frontier-level quality — Gemini Flash or GPT-4o Mini can be 10-50x cheaper
