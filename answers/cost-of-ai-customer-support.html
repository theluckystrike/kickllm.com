How Much Does AI Customer Support Cost Per Ticket?

AI customer support costs $0.002-$0.02 per ticket with budget models (Gemini Flash, GPT-4o Mini) or $0.02-$0.15 per ticket with premium models. This is 10-50x cheaper than human agents at $5-15 per ticket.

Recommended Architecture

  • Tier 1 — Instant (80% of tickets): GPT-4o Mini or Gemini Flash for FAQ-style queries. Cost: ~$0.001/ticket
  • Tier 2 — Complex (15% of tickets): Claude Sonnet 4 for nuanced issues. Cost: ~$0.014/ticket
  • Tier 3 — Human (5% of tickets): Escalate to human agents for edge cases. Cost: ~$10/ticket
  • Blended cost: ~$0.50-$1.00 per ticket at scale

FAQ

What is the cheapest AI for customer support?

Gemini 2.0 Flash at $0.075/$0.30 per 1M tokens is the cheapest option from a major provider. At ~$0.0003 per ticket, you can handle 100,000 tickets/month for under $30.

Can AI fully replace human support agents?

AI can handle 70-90% of common support queries. Complex issues, emotional situations, and edge cases still benefit from human agents. A tiered approach (AI first, human escalation) is most cost-effective.

How accurate is AI customer support?

With proper RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) setup and quality knowledge base, AI support achieves 85-95% accuracy on common questions. Quality improves with better training data and prompt engineering.
